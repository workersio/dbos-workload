## Classification

Finding.

The target preserves queued async tasks against GC while pending, but public
workflow cancellation can mark the durable rows terminal without cancelling or
releasing the underlying in-process async workflow tasks. This violates the
E-019 cleanup side of the product promise: terminal cancelled workflows should
not remain pinned in `_workflow_tasks` until runtime teardown.

The first violated branch is the cancellation cleanup case. The modeled
success branches and no-GeneratorExit checks reached their expected state
before the leak invariant failed.
